Castle Defender

  • Originality: It is based on Defender type games.
  • Technical Merit: You are using a shield to protect the castle.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? I learned how to use collide with more then one item. What was the easiest or hardest part of making it? The hardest part was making the cannon balls have different behaviors.

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game is like any other defender base game.
  • High Concept: Denfend your castle.
  • Theme: None
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype evaluats the player movement and how fast the player can react.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player will experience the feel of anticipation of what is going to happen next.
  • Gameplay: The player can not be hurt by the cannon balls, but the castle can. So the player must destory all the canon balls before they get to the castle. The castle can only take damage of five cannon balls.
  • Strategies: The player knows that the cannon balls come from one side, so the player can just stay close has possible to that side.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Your home is under attack by these new developed cannon balls and you must protect your home if possible. These new cannon balls bounce of balls and do a lot of damaged, but you have developed a new shield made from vibranium and it can not only stop the cannon ball but destroy it. You must protect your people.
  • Target Audience: The target audience is kids that like defender games.
  • Play Time: The game time is as longs as the player can survive.
